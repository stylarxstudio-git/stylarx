Open Tracking
How it works: We embed a 1×1 invisible tracking pixel in each HTML email — the same method used by Mailchimp, HubSpot, and Superhuman. When the recipient opens the email, their client loads the pixel and we mark it as opened.

Accuracy: Works reliably on Gmail (desktop + Android), Outlook, and most web clients. Apple Mail on iOS 15+ has Mail Privacy Protection which pre-fetches images — those will always show as "opened" even if the person hasn't read it. There is no workaround for this. On average, expect ~85–90% accuracy across a typical email list.
